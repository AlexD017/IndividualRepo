SUV

Short for Sports Utility Vehicle, SUVs are cars that combine both road cars and off road automobiles. There are multiple sizes for most SUVs.

RV

Recreational Vehicles are very popular among tourists. They mostly consist of a small kitchen, bathroom, living room, and bedroom. Some are even used as motorhomes.

Jeep

Jeeps are generally characterized by strong engines, big tires, and an advantage on off road trails or during wild vacations.

Convertible

Convertibles are characterized by a roof that opens and closes as desired. They are more expensive as luxury cars and mostly seen in cities over rural areas.

Truck

Most trucks have stronger engines than normal cars with an open trunk for storage. When on vacation in the woods, these are excellent for space division and easy navigation.
